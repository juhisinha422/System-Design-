## Overview

These blogs cover essential concepts of system design and distributed systems that will help you understand how modern architectures function at scale. From understanding how caching works, to the intricacies of load balancing, microservices, and data consistency, these topics will solidify your foundational knowledge.

## Why System Design?

System design is a crucial aspect of software engineering, especially for building scalable, reliable, and maintainable applications. A strong understanding of system design principles will help you approach complex problems, break them down into manageable components, and optimize your solutions. These concepts are also frequently encountered in technical interviews for roles like Software Engineers, Backend Engineers, and Cloud Architects.

## How to Use This Resource

- Start with a topic that interests you or aligns with your current learning goals.
- Read the blog and implement any suggested strategies or tools.
- Build side projects or exercises to practice what you’ve learned.
- Continue progressing through the list to reinforce and expand your knowledge.
- Use the topics in interviews or discussions with peers to further solidify your understanding.
